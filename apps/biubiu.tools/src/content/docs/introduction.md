# Introduction

**BiuBiu Tools** is a collection of focused, self-custodial utilities for everyday
on-chain work — checking what can move your tokens, scanning balances across
chains, sending to thousands of addresses, exploring networks, and more.

Everything runs in your browser, most tools need no sign-up, and your keys never
leave your device. The goal is simple: take the repetitive, risky parts of using
Web3 and make them calm and obvious.

## Principles

- **Self-custodial.** You hold your keys. BiuBiu cannot move, freeze, or recover
  your funds. Tools that sign do so with your wallet, in front of you.
- **Privacy-first.** Work happens client-side wherever possible. We avoid
  collecting data we don't need, and many tools require no account at all.
- **No blind signing.** Transactions are shown in human-readable terms before you
  approve them — never a wall of hex.
- **Free and open.** The toolkit is [open source on GitHub](https://github.com/atshelchin/biubiu-monorepo)
  so anyone can audit exactly what each tool does.
- **Multi-chain by default.** Tools work across the major EVM networks, plus any
  custom chain you add.

## What's inside

A few of the tools you'll find:

- **[Revoke Approvals](/docs/revoke-approvals)** — find every contract that can
  still move your tokens and NFTs, then shut off the risky ones.
- **[Balance Radar](/docs/balance-radar)** — bulk-check balances for thousands of
  addresses across chains and export a report.
- **[Token Sender](/docs/token-sender)** — batch-send a coin or token to many
  recipients from your own wallet.
- **[Chain Explorer](/docs/chain-explorer)** — browse 800+ networks, find RPCs and
  explorers, and add a chain to your wallet in one click.
